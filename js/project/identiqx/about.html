Our story

We're building the identity wallet
we've always wanted.

IdentiqX started with a simple question: why is it so hard to keep the documents that matter most, right where we can find them?

Why we built IdentiqX

The everyday problems nobody solves well.

We looked at how people actually manage identity documents today. It's messy. Here's what we saw — and what IdentiqX fixes.

Documents scattered everywhere

Across email, WhatsApp, drive folders, and photo galleries.

Slow, painful searching

Trying to find that one PDF exactly when you need it most.

Privacy concerns

Sensitive IDs uploaded to services you don't fully trust.

Cloud dependency

No internet? Suddenly you can't access what's yours.

Paper documents

Fragile, easy to misplace, impossible to search.

Poor experiences

Existing wallets feel like forms, not products you love.

The IdentiqX answer

One private, offline-first, beautifully organized vault — for everything that proves who you are.

Mission

Give every person a private, delightful home for their most important documents.

Vision
