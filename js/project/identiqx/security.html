Trust by design

Your identity,
absolutely private.

Security isn't a marketing checkbox. It's the foundation IdentiqX is built on. Here's exactly how we protect what matters most.

Eight security pillars

Protection you can actually verify.

Every claim below reflects how IdentiqX is architected — not what we hope to add later.

AES-256 Encryption

Every stored document is encrypted at rest with 256-bit AES — the same standard trusted by governments and banks.

Biometric Authentication

Unlock with fingerprint or face — powered by your device's secure enclave, never sent anywhere.

PIN Protection

A dedicated PIN adds a second layer, so even device unlock isn't enough to reach your vault.

Offline Storage

By default, nothing leaves your device. No servers to breach, no accounts to compromise.

Secure Local Database

Documents live in an encrypted local database with hardware-backed key storage.

No Tracking

No analytics SDKs, no session recording, no third-party trackers. We can't see what you store.

No Ads. No Selling.

Your data is never sold, rented, or monetized. This is a principle, not a policy.

Future E2EE Backup

When cloud backup arrives, it's end-to-end encrypted and fully opt-in — you hold the keys.

A promise, in plain words.

We don't want your data. We can't read it, we won't sell it, and we don't need it to run our business. IdentiqX exists so you own your identity — not us, not advertisers, not anyone else.
